What is BenchCalc?
BenchCalc is a pocket reference and calculator built for scientists at the bench. It works entirely offline, has no ads, no tracking, and no account required. One-time purchase, everything included.
49 Tools Across 8 Categories
Nucleic Acids - Mass/moles converter, A260 concentration, Tm prediction with primer pair comparison and dimer checking, primer resuspension from lyophilised tubes, agarose gel pouring with TAE/TBE and stain volumes.
Protein Tools - Molecular weight from sequence, Bradford/BCA assay with standard curve or quick estimate, Beer-Lambert A280, peptide isoelectric point and charge calculator, enzyme unit converter, PAGE gel pouring for SDS-PAGE/native/denaturing gels.
Dilutions & Solutions - C1V1=C2V2 dilution, serial dilution planner, molarity calculator, percent solutions (w/v, v/v, w/w), normality with N1V1=N2V2, resuspension calculator.
Buffers & Reagents - Buffer preparation, Henderson-Hasselbalch solver, stock solution preparation, multi-solute buffer builder with built-in reagent database.
Cell & Lab Tools - Cell counting, seeding density, centrifuge RCF/RPM converter, doubling time, radioactive decay with preset isotopes and decay curve, lab timer with 28 presets.
PCR & Cloning - Master mix with enzyme presets and 10% overage, restriction digest with up to 3 enzymes and automatic buffer compatibility/methylation/star activity checking, ligation with enzyme presets and molar ratios, general reaction mix with C1V1=C2V2 scaling.
Sequence Analysis - DNA/RNA/protein translator with auto-detection, reverse complement, six reading frames with ORF highlighting, feature annotation (start/stop codons, Shine-Dalgarno, promoters, Kozak, poly-A signals) with prokaryotic/eukaryotic toggle.
Utilities - Unit converter with full SI ladder (femto to peta) across 7 categories, quick calculator with expression history and memory, lab timer with multiple simultaneous countdowns.
11 Step-Through Protocols
Miniprep, ethanol precipitation, gel extraction, PCR cleanup, restriction digest, ligation, heat shock transformation, E. coli growth & harvest, affinity column purification, column maintenance, and inclusion body purification. Each protocol has checklist and focus modes, duration badges, volume chips, warnings, and tips.
8 Reference Tables

20 amino acids - structures, MW, pI, pKa, pH-dependent protonation, charge display with pH slider
5 nucleotide bases - structures, properties, IUPAC degenerate codes
64 codons - standard genetic code, searchable
132 restriction enzymes - Type II, IIS, nicking, homing endonucleases with methylation sensitivity, star activity, buffer info, compatible end matching
284 lab reagents - MW, formula, CAS, working concentrations, storage, solubility, hazards
20 antibiotics & selection agents - working concentrations, stock prep, resistance genes, mechanism of action
15 media & buffer recipes - scalable recipes for LB, SOC, SOB, TB, 2xYT, M9, PBS, TBS, TAE, TBE, and more
60 fluorophores & dyes - excitation/emission, spectrum bars, Stokes shift, laser line compatibility filter
